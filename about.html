About The Mawakeb Mediks

Founded by students with a shared passion for medicine and community impact, we empower members to explore healthcare through projects, events, and mentorship.

Club history

Started in 2023 with 12 members, we’ve grown into a cross‑grade community shaping health initiatives on campus.

What we value

Integrity, curiosity, inclusion, and evidence‑based thinking.
